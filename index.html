SUBJECT: BUDGET CHANGES

Dear Washington University Students,


At WashU, we recognize that our community drives everything we do and stand for. We pride ourselves on our supportive and outstanding campus culture, which is only possible through the contributions of your achievements and character. Thus, we have made the executive decision to give back to the WashU community by reallocating excess printing funds to the endowment in order to better serve future generations. Rest assured that this was not an easy decision, but one that we felt would best serve the interests of our community.


Starting this year, each student will now be provided with $1.00 in printing money to use as they see fit. We hope this helps foster independence and wisdom as you learn to navigate these changes.


Sincerely,


Andrew D. Martin

Chancellor
